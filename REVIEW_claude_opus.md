## Top-level takeaways for Juan

1. **Schema is ~70% sound** but has structural issues: `wallet_profile` is a
   single mutable row (lookahead bug), `liquidity_locked BOOLEAN` is a footgun,
   `bounce_rate` won't compute on raw `fact_trade` at scale, and there is no
   `dim_token_authority` — the single biggest rug predictor.

2. **The 7-signal ensemble is premature.** Start with 3 hard gates:
   - mint/freeze authority not renounced
   - top1 LP share of pool > 50%
   - sniper bundle in slot 0
   Plus one soft score. Validate on 30 of your own historical LP positions
   *before* adding more signals.

3. **You need `fact_lp_position` to close the loop.** Without a table of your
   own positions, you can't tell if any signal actually predicted your losses.
   That table is what makes the whole system pay rent.

4. **Validation must be time-aware.** "Run on 20–30 tokens" is too vague —
   compute signals at `first_pool_at + {1h, 6h, 24h}` and measure AUC at
   each decision window. The 1h AUC and the 24h AUC will be very different.

5. **Watch the circular dependency**: `wallet_profile.label` feeds
   `token_signals.insider_wallet_count`, which feeds `token_risk_score`,
   which (if joined back) re-feeds `wallet_profile`. Snapshot the wallet
   profile as-of `first_pool_at` to kill this.
